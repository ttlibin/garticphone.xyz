About Gartic Phone

Gartic Phone is a free online multiplayer drawing and guessing web game developed and published by Onrizon. Released in December 2020, the game quickly gained massive popularity across streaming platforms like Twitch and YouTube, becoming a favorite among content creators and casual players alike.

The game's unique concept combines the classic "telephone" game with digital drawing, creating hilarious chains of misinterpretation that entertain players and viewers. Its accessibility, social nature, and creative gameplay have contributed to its widespread appeal.

Key aspects of Gartic Phone include:

  • Free-to-play browser-based gameplay
  • Support for 4-30 players per session
  • Multiple game modes and customization options
  • Cross-platform compatibility
  • No account required for basic play
  • Regular updates and new features from the developers
