Gartic Phone FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Can you play Gartic Phone with just two players?

It's technically possible, but the game shines with larger groups. With 4–30 players, the chain of drawings and guesses becomes more complex and entertaining.

Is Gartic Phone completely free?

Yes. Gartic Phone is free to play with no subscription requirements. All core features are accessible to everyone.

How long do typical sessions last?

Most sessions last between 10 and 30 minutes, depending on the number of participants and chosen settings. This makes it easy to play several rounds in one session.

Can I play Gartic Phone on mobile?

Absolutely. Gartic Phone is fully compatible with mobile browsers and supports intuitive touch controls for drawing.

Do I need an account to play?

No account is required to start playing. You can join instantly with a nickname. Optional accounts unlock features like game history and profile customization.

What makes Gartic Phone different?

Gartic Phone connects players' drawings and guesses in a chain, creating an evolving narrative that leads to unexpected and hilarious results every round.
