What is Gartic Phone?

Gartic Phone is a free online multiplayer drawing and guessing game that blends creativity and comedy. Players write prompts, turn them into drawings, and others try to interpret those drawings back into words. As the chain progresses through multiple participants, the original message evolves into something unexpectedly hilarious.

This unique Gartic Phone gameplay loop guarantees every round feels fresh and memorable, making it perfect for friends, family game nights, streamers, and team-building sessions.
